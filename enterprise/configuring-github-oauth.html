Configuring GitHub OAuth

Step 1: Register a new OAuth application

  1. On yourdomain.com, sign into any account that is on the Owners team of your company's organization.

  2. In the upper-right corner of any page, click the Account Settings icon.

  3. In the left sidebar, click the name of your organization.

  4. In the left sidebar, click Applications.

  5. At the top of the page, under Organization applications, click Register new application.

  6. Under Application name, type:


    Gitcolony Enterprise

  7. Under Homepage URL, type the URL of your Gitcolony Enterprise.

    https://gitcolony.yourcompany.com

  8. Under Authorization callback URL, type the URL of your Gitcolony Enterprise followed by /users/auth/github/callback.

    https://gitcolony.yourcompany.com/users/auth/github/callback

  9. Click Register application.

  10. Note the "Client ID" and "Client Secret" values that appear (you will need them later).
